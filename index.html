How I Built Eco‑Pulse

Take a behind‑the‑scenes look at how I designed and built the Eco‑Pulse app from scratch.

Eco‑Pulse encourages sustainable habits. Users track their carbon footprint from transport, energy use and food choices. The app then gives personalised suggestions, graphs progress, and offers eco‑challenges like “Plastic‑Free Day”. Built with SwiftUI and a lightweight backend, the design stays clean and fun.
