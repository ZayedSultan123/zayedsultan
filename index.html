How I Built Eco-Pulse

Take a behind-the-scenes look at how I designed and built the Eco-Pulse app from scratch.

Eco-Pulse helps users make eco-friendly travel decisions by calculating carbon savings. Just choose your start and destination, select a green travel option like cycling or electric car, and the app instantly draws the route on a live map. It displays your total distance, estimated time, and how much carbon you’re saving — helping you turn every trip from Abu Dhabi to Dubai (or anywhere) into a win for the environment.
